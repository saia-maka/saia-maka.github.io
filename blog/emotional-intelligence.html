Emotional Intelligence?

What is emotion intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is an individual's ability to perceive, control and evaluate emotions in themselves and to understand, interpret and respond to the emotions of others.

How is it different to IQ?

EQ focuses on your emotional intelligence rather than how intelligent you are mentally. The difference can be thought of in many different ways, however one that helps me understand the difference is: IQ can be thought of as how 'smart' an individual is whereas EQ can be thought of as how 'mature' an individual is. IQ is a measure of our reasoning/problem solving ability to think logically. EQ is a measure of our ability to perceive, understand/evaluate and respond to our or other's emotions.

Why is it important?

Improving EQ will help improve your health and well-being. It will help to you think before reacting, help to manage yourself safely and more effectively, help you to manage others safely and to show empathy, help you to deal with challenging situations more effectively and ultimately it will build a greater sense of self-awareness. These will improve how you interact and engage with yourself and others and will develop your leadership/teamworking skills.
