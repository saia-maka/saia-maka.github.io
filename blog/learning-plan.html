Foundations learning plan

Saia's learning plan for Mataī Dev Academy 2022

My long term goal is to become a software engineer and to run a business developing/distributing software and web applications.

I like to think that I keep an open mind with most things in life. I'm always open to feedback and I'm open to different points of views which I believe helps me adjust to different environments, different personalities, learning new things or adopting new beliefs, and also helps to challenge my own knowledge and beliefs. I'm also quite a humble person which I feel is a really great strength but sometimes I know I deflect praise and as I've learnt already in foundations is that I need to accept it as it will help build my confidence. I have a great work ethic and can persevere during hard times. To assist that I need to work on organising myself more and not getting distracted so that I can get the most out of my learning and bring the best out of myself.

As I have mentioned I struggle to reach out for help, however I will commit myself to growing out of this habit/behaviour. When I need help or when I don't understand something I will put my hand up or I will ask for help from other students/facilitators in a timely way (after I've had a decent go myself), usually on discord or otherwise in person.

I would love to develop my organisation skills as I believe it is key to maintaining success in learning, work and other areas of my life. I would also love to develop my shyness/openness to others. I struggle to reach out to people who can help, especially creating conversation with others and putting myself out there, and how to interact with others in a social setting. This is a huge piece that I feel I've been missing and struggling with for a long time so I would really appreciate working on this. I would also love to really step outside my comfort zone to work on my leadership skills - practice mentoring and being mentored, helping others learn and to practice taking a leadership role. Finally, I would love to work on my teamworking skills as working in a team is essential to working as a developer and will set me up for success in different teams in the future.

It is my hope the facilitators can continue to help me adjust and prime myself to reach my goals, to set myself up for success in terms on staying on the right track with my study/work and to get the most out of this learning experience. Whether it be in the form of feedback, a casual conversation and or extra learning material they believe or can see is relevant to what I need, I'm more than happy to take it on board. I'll take any help I can get and will keep an open-mind.

I do have whanau commitments as I'm a father but they are usually taken care of outside of my working hours or by my partner. If for some reason I need to take care of something or need to be excused I will let the appropriate people know as soon as I can.

I will schedule specific times for myself for when I'm working and when I'm not. For example, times I will start for the day, times I will finish, and days I will be working. My family around me will be made aware and reminded of this commitment to help support and prime myself and my environment to enable me to work productively, safely and to the best of my ability. If and when I can help other students, I will make a decent effort to do so. If and when I need assistance from or to report to facilitators, I will also make a decent effort to do so. I will not put the safety of others at risk, I will treat others with respect and do my best to put my best foot forward at every stage of my learning.

I will take brief moments to breath and calm myself to manage lesser challenging intensities of emotions or stress. For more challenging intensities I will take a timeout to step away, dust myself off, get some air into me, talk to a facilitator or call a support person or two, then return and communicate how I was feeling and why I had to step away. Otherwise, I will communicate to my facilitators how Im feeling and seek advice/assistance where appropriate.

Bootcamp learning plan

What is your long-term goal and/or career pathway?

My vision is that the skills I gain during this course will get my foot in the door as a full-stack developer in a prominent tech company. So that I may be able to gain exposure and further development to help me progress as a developer into an eventual senior developer position. I believe this pathway will lead me to my long-term goal of starting a tech business developing and deploying software and apps.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My biggest strengths will be my past coding experience with Take2, my growth mindset, and the problem solving skills ive been developing in foundations. My limitations have changed as I now believe my biggest limitations are my ego and the self-critical voice inside my head. If I'm not careful to build on these weaknesses it will reflect in my interaction with my family, facilitators, and peers, and stunt the growth that the dev academy experience has the potential for.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I think my biggest non-technical challenge will be to manage and balance my life. I have 7 children, a wife, myself, studies, and boot camp to balance over the next 9 weeks. A step further into that is my time management and organization skills. This will be an epic challenge!

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I will like to develop my presentation skills: Being precise in speaking/listening with others, being able to project my voice, using gestures effectively, and confidence in the way I present.

I will like to develop my leadership and teamworking skills: Being able to help and help others problem-solve/learn. How to help others grow and how to facilitate that by the way I interact and work with them. To understand how to build that kind of relationship with someone and develop my understanding of what a leader is and on the flipped side how to be an efficient team worker. How to work with others and how to enhance the culture of a team or add value to a team. How to build a team and how to help a team to succeed.

I will like to develop my networking and entrepreneurial skills: How to build connections and build my network, how to approach other professionals and build relationships with them, how to strategize and sell products, and how to build business sense/skills.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I just hope they can keep pushing me in the right direction, help me to grow and better myself, and help me succeed.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

I will do my best to complete all my work to the best of my ability.
I will wake and sleep at appropriate times to prime myself for learning and I will commit my time to staying on top of my workload.
I will take regular breaks, stick to a schedule, and continue to review my learning plan.
I will respect facilitators, representatives, my peers, as well as views that differ from my own.
I will not put the safety of others at risk and I will show empathy, kindness, and effort at all times.
